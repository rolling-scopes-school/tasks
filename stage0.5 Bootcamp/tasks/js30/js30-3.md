# Vertical Slider

## Skills

`DOM API` `CSS transforms` `CSS transitions` `event handling` `wheel events` `pointer events`

## Task Description

**Vertical Slider** is a two-column slider where text and image columns scroll vertically in opposite directions when the user clicks the up / down arrows. Sliders are a workhorse component on landing pages, portfolios, product pages, and image galleries - and they come in many flavours.

- Parent task and scoring: [js30.md](js30.md)

## Mandatory Additional Feature

Make the slider **infinite (looped)**: after the last slide the next click jumps back to the first one, and going backwards from the first slide lands on the last. The transition should look natural - no visible "rewind" jump. [Reference infinite-slider demo](https://codepen.io/atulb29007/full/LzPgaE)

## Optional Improvements

Each well-executed item below is worth **+10** points (Stage 3 in [js30.md](js30.md), capped at 30 per widget). You may also invent your own improvements of comparable complexity.

- Mouse-wheel navigation - scrolling the wheel over the slider moves to the next / previous slide. [Reference](https://codepen.io/suez/full/gadLre)
- Touch / pointer swipe navigation - drag-and-release advances the slider, with a velocity-aware fling. [Reference](https://codepen.io/mrspok407/full/bwLwvL)
- A switchable orientation - the same slider can be flipped from vertical to horizontal at runtime. [Reference](https://codepen.io/simonpatrat/full/zeGYdo)
- Replace static slides with **audio and video** slides - a media-aware slider.
