## FAQ

#### I cannot copy or edit the layout. What should I do?

To edit the layout, you need to copy it to your account. Follow these steps:
click on the layout name in the center at the top, select "duplicate to your drafts", a black button will appear at the bottom - click open
Video tutorial on how to do this - https://yadi.sk/i/wd7Z9cP2VbFxgg
