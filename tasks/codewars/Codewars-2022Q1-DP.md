## Как сабмитить задание

Codewars is an auto-checked task.
After completing the task, go to RS App <https://app.rs.school/>, select **Auto-Test**, select **Codewars-2022Q1-DP** from the dropdown list, and click the **Submit** button. You’ll see the result on the right.

You can submit the assignment as many times as you like; each subsequent submission overwrites the previous one.

Don't forget to change your nickname on Codewars. The nickname will be displayed when selecting the **Codewars-2022Q1-DP** task on RS App. To change your nickname on Codewars follow the link <https://www.codewars.com/users/edit>

## Scoring criteria

- 1 point for 8 kyu, 7 kyu and Beta tasks (3 tasks in total)
- 4 points - 6 kyu (5 in total)
- 8 points - 4 kyu (1 in total)

Total: 1 \* 3 + 4 \* 5 + 8 \* 1 = 31

**Total sum: 31 points.**
