## How to Submit the Assignment

Codewars is an auto-checked task.  
After completing the assignment, go to rs app https://app.rs.school/, select **Auto-Test**, choose **Codewars Part 1** from the dropdown list, and click the **Submit** button. The verification result will appear on the right.

You can submit the assignment as many times as you want, each subsequent submit overwrites the previous one.

Don't forget to change your nickname on Codewars. It will be indicated when selecting the **Codewars Part 1** task in rs app. You can change your nickname on codewars at https://www.codewars.com/users/edit

## Scoring Criteria
