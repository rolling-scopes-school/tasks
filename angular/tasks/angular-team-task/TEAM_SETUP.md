# Team Setup

## Team size

Teams must consist of **2–3 students**.

> **Recommendation:** It is preferred to form a team with students who share the same mentor. This simplifies communication, code reviews, and sprint interviews, since the mentor is already familiar with everyone's progress.

---

## Step 1 — Find teammates

Post your team-up request in the Discord channel **#students-looking-for-team**:
[Open channel](https://discordapp.com/channels/788026364372320256/1497988253272379504)

### Post format

```text
Name:
Location (Country/City):
Communication languages:
GitHub profile link:
Telegram profile link:
About me / tech background:
```

---

## Step 2 — Create a team in RS App

1. Open [RS App](https://app.rs.school/) and go to the **Team Distribution** tab.
2. Create a new team and add all team members.
3. Make sure every member of your team is listed before submitting.
